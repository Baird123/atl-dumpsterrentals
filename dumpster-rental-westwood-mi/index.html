Frequently Asked Questions

Can I rent a dumpster for a single day?

Yes, our rental periods are flexible, allowing for single-day or extended rentals to meet your project needs.

What items cannot be placed in the dumpster?

Hazardous materials, chemicals, propane tanks, and certain electronics are restricted. Our team provides a complete list during rental.

Do you provide permits for placing dumpsters on the street?

Yes, we assist with any necessary local permits for street placement.

How does delivery and pickup work?

You schedule delivery, clear the area, load your debris, then request pickup when done. Many companies allow you to be off-site during delivery and pickup.

Are there weight or size restrictions for dumpsters?

Yes each dumpster size has a weight limit. Heavy materials like concrete or dirt may require special bins or excess weight fees.

What if my project generates more debris than expected?

You can upgrade to a larger dumpster or schedule an extra pickup. We’ll help you adjust if your scale changes.

Westwood, MI Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Westwood is an unincorporated community in Kalamazoo Charter Township, Kalamazoo County in the U.S. state of Michigan. It is a census-designated place (CDP) for statistical purposes and does not have any legal status as an incorporated municipality. The population was 8,653 at the 2010 census, down from 9,122 at the 2000 census.
